## 🚀 Featured Projects

### [Torres Strait Water Dynamics: A Remote Sensing Analysis](https://github.com/jessburrowsGEO/torres-strait-water-mapping)
**Spatial Landscape Assessment | Earth Observation | Python**

Investigated the relationship between monthly rainfall and surface water extent on Badu and Horn Islands (1995–2022) to support **UN Sustainable Development Goal 6.6.1**.

*   **The Problem:** Remote island communities are vulnerable when it comes to fresh water supply. Understanding the rainfall and hydrology of individual Islands may shed light on potential solutions for fresh water assessments in terms of availability and quality.
*   **The Tech:** Utilised a Python workflow in the **Digital Earth Australia (DEA) Sandbox** using **Sentinel-2 Analysis Ready Data (ARD)**. 
*   **The Analysis:** Calculated **MNDWI** (Modified Normalized Difference Water Index) to delineate water bodies and performed temporal correlation analysis with BOM rainfall data.
*   **The Insight:** Identified a clear lagged correlation in managed systems (Horn Island Dam) but highlighted critical detection challenges in tropical ephemeral systems due to cloud cover and vegetation.

`Python` `Digital Earth Australia` `Remote Sensing` `Water Resource Management` `GIS`
